Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about compatibility, installation, and delivery.

Is the Vier Kit compatible with my bicycle?

The Vier Kit is designed to fit most standard bicycle frames with a 68mm to 73mm bottom bracket shell. This covers the vast majority of mountain bikes, road bikes, and commuter bikes. It is not compatible with bikes that use proprietary or non-standard bottom brackets (like press-fit or fat bikes).

How difficult is the installation process?

We designed the Vier Kit for easy, DIY installation. If you can replace a crankset, you can install this kit. On average, users complete the installation in under an hour. We provide all necessary custom tools and clear, step-by-step video guides (see the Installation section).

What is the warranty period for the kit?

The Vier Kit motor and battery are covered by a comprehensive 2-year warranty against manufacturing defects. The warranty covers parts and labor for repair or replacement. Display and accessories are covered for one year.

When will the pre-ordered kits be shipped?

Kits purchased in the current Batch 1 pre-order window are scheduled to complete final assembly and quality checks by Q1 2026, with final delivery estimated for Early Q2 2026. Please refer to the Campaign Timeline for more details.
