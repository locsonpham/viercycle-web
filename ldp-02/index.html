VIER KIT isn’t here to replace your ride — it’s here to empower it. With smart pedal assist, you’re still in control — every push of the pedal is yours, but VIER KIT gives you just the right boost to keep your ride smooth and effortless. It helps protect your knees, reduce fatigue, and maintain a steady heart rate, so you can go farther, climb higher, and enjoy more rides without feeling worn out. This isn’t about doing less. It’s about riding longer, stronger, and smarter.
